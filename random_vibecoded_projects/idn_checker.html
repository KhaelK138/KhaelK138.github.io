IDN Domain Lookalike Analyzer

Check IDN registrability & discover lookalike phishing domains for .com/.net

About this tool

Based on IANA .com Latin IDN table (com_latn_1.2.txt, Verisign) and Unicode UTS #39 confusables data. Checks registrability and generates potential lookalike domains ranked by visual similarity score.

Techniques: ASCII multi-char lookalikes (rn→m, vv→w, cl→d), character swaps (l↔1, o↔0), Latin Extended substitution (ogonek, cedilla, Vietnamese horn — Chrome-verified), and character repetition.

Evidence sources: RaccoonO365 campaign (rnicrosoft.com, 5K+ victims across 94 countries).
